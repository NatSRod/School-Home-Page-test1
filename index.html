Wee School Preschool inc.

Of Erie, Colorado

Preparing Students for Academic Success

The Wee School Preschool is a family owned preschool located in the heart of Erie, CO.

Licensed by the State of Colorado, we offer preschool 3s and Kinder prep (4-5 years old). The Wee School Preschool is an academic based preschool with an after school care option. We offer a safe, warm learning environment, which enables children to succeed in their academic, social and physical development, thus enhancing self esteem and creating a positive early learning experience.

The Wee School focuses on two main objectives:

Provide academic instruction: we focus on literacy (speaking, listening, reading and writing) instruction, which is age appropriate and meets the needs of the children. We also provide instruction in the areas of math, science, social studies, art and spanish.

Enrich the whole child: we strive to educate the child holistically, including their social emotional well being with the main goal of the child's long term success.
